Hi! I am a PhD student studying Computational Mathematics, Science, and Engineering (CMSE) at [Michigan State University](https://cmse.msu.edu/) supervised by Drs. [Andrew Christlieb](https://the-christlieb-group.github.io/) and [Brian O'Shea](https://web.pa.msu.edu/people/osheabr/index.html). I am a member of the PSAAP Focused Investigatory Center for [High Order Plasma Turbulence Modeling for Z-Pinch (HighZ)](https://highz-fic.github.io/) and a trainee in the [NSF-funded AIDMM-NRT Program](https://cmse.msu.edu/Academics/AIDMM-NRT.aspx). My current research interest focuses on applying scientific machine learning to computational plasma physics.
